Neem

Neem is a tropical tree known for its health benefits.

Region: Tropical

Common Names: English: Neem Hindi: Neem Spanish: Nim

Type: Tree

Habitat: Thrives in dry, arid regions. Prefers well-drained sandy or loamy soils.

Botanical Name: Azadirachta indica

Medicinal Uses: Antibacterial: Effective against various bacteria and fungi. Anti-inflammatory: Reduces inflammation and pain. Skin Health: Used in skin care for acne and eczema. Blood Sugar Regulation: May help regulate blood sugar levels.

Methods of Cultivation: Climate Requirements: Grows best in hot, dry climates. Soil Requirements: Thrives in well-drained soils, tolerant of poor soil conditions. Propagation: Propagated through seeds or cuttings. Irrigation: Requires minimal watering; drought-resistant. Pest and Disease Control: Naturally resistant to most pests. Harvesting; Leaves and seeds can be harvested; leaves are often used fresh or dried.
